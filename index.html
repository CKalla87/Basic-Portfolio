About Me

My Name is Christopher Anthony Kalla. I like anything music. I started playing guitar when I was 10 years old and have never stopped learning and playing new instruments.

When I began playing my passion grew to allow me to join a group of friends and start playing out live. This later led me to travel the United States and share my music with others. This was one of the best experiences of my life!
